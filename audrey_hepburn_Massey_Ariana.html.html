Early Life

Audrey Hepburn was born in Brussels, Belgium. Her given name was Edda Kathleen van Heemstra Hepburn. Her childhood was marked by World War II, during which she experienced hardship and witnessed the impact of the Nazi occupation.

Acting Career

Hepburn's acting career blossomed after the war. She is celebrated for iconic roles in films like *Roman Holiday*, *Breakfast at Tiffany's*, and *My Fair Lady*. Her elegance, grace, and acting talent made her a beloved figure in Hollywood.
